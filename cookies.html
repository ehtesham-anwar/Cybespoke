Cookie policy

Last updated: 9 May 2026

This policy explains how Cybespoke uses cookies and similar technologies on cybespoke.co.uk. It should be read alongside our Privacy notice.

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, to improve performance, and to provide information to the site owner. We also use a small number of similar technologies, such as local storage, which we treat as cookies for the purposes of this policy.

Our approach

We try to use as few cookies as we reasonably can. We will only set non‑essential cookies (for example, analytics) where you have given us your consent. You can withdraw your consent at any time using the controls described below.

Cookies we use

CookiePurposeTypeDuration
cybespoke_session Keeps your session secure while you use forms on this website. Strictly necessary Session
cybespoke_consent Remembers your cookie preferences so we do not ask again on every visit. Strictly necessary 12 months
_pa_* (privacy‑friendly analytics) Anonymous, aggregated information about how visitors use the site, so we can improve it. Does not track you across other websites. Analytics (consent‑based) Up to 24 months

We do not use advertising cookies, profiling cookies, or any third‑party tracking pixels for marketing purposes.

Managing your preferences

  • On this site — when you first visit we ask whether you accept optional analytics cookies. You can change your decision at any time by clearing the cybespoke_consent cookie in your browser, after which we will ask again.
  • In your browser — most browsers let you block or delete cookies entirely. Useful guides are available at aboutcookies.org and allaboutcookies.org.
  • "Do Not Track" — if your browser sends a Do Not Track signal, we treat it as a refusal of optional analytics cookies.

Please note that disabling strictly necessary cookies may prevent parts of the website (for example, contact forms) from functioning properly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of the page will always show when it was last revised.

Questions

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please email hello@cybespoke.co.uk. Our hours are 09:00 – 17:00, Monday to Friday (UK time).
