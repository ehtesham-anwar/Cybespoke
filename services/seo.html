Common questions

The honest answers people ask us most.

How long does SEO take to work?

You'll see small wins in the first month — speed, titles, Google Business Profile. Real ranking shifts usually take three to six months. Anyone promising "page one in a fortnight" is selling you something else.

Do you tie us into long contracts?

No. Everything is month‑to‑month. We'd rather earn next month than lock you in.

Will you write the content for us?

Yes. We write SEO articles, landing‑page copy and ad copy in‑house, in plain English, in your tone — not stuffed full of keywords.

What's included in the free audit?

A 6 to 10‑page written report covering site speed, on‑page SEO, technical issues, content gaps and your local presence — plus a prioritised list of fixes and a clear price for each plan.

Do we need a new website to work with you?

Not always. We start with what you've got. If your current site is fundamentally holding you back, we'll say so — and we can rebuild it (see our Websites service).

Will we own everything you do?

Yes. All accounts, content, tracking and configuration belong to you. If you ever leave us, you keep everything.
