How we work together

A quiet, deliberate process.

Step i

The conversation

A 60-minute call. We listen. We push. By the end, both sides know whether bespoke is the right answer — or whether something off-the-shelf will serve you better.

Step ii

The proposal

One page. The problem we agreed on, the path we recommend, the price, the dates, and the names of the people who'll do the work. No decks.

Step iii

The build

Weekly demos. A staging URL from week one. Direct line to engineers. Senior people on the keyboard, not just on the kickoff.

Step iv

The handover

Documentation, walk-throughs, a 30-day warranty, and an open invitation to come back. Many do.
