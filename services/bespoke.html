How we work together

A measured, considered process.

Step 1

The conversation

An initial discussion to understand your objectives, constraints and context. By the end, we will both know whether a bespoke approach is right — or whether an existing solution would serve you better.

Step 2

The proposal

A concise, single‑page document setting out the problem, our recommended approach, the investment, the timeline and the team who will deliver the work. No slide decks.

Step 3

The build

Weekly demonstrations, a staging environment from the first week and direct access to the engineers building your software. Senior practitioners on the work, not solely at the kick‑off.

Step 4

The handover

Comprehensive documentation, structured walk‑throughs, a 30‑day warranty and a standing invitation to return. The majority of our clients do.
