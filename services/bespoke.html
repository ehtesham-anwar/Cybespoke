How a bespoke engagement runs

A measured, considered process.

Step 1

Discovery & consultation

We begin with an unhurried conversation to understand your organisation, your customers and the commercial outcome you intend to achieve. By its conclusion, we will both know with certainty whether a bespoke build is genuinely the correct course of action, or whether an existing product would serve you more sensibly.

Step 2

Bespoke proposal & scope

A single, plainly written document. It sets out the problem we have agreed, the bespoke approach we recommend, a transparent breakdown of the investment, a realistic timetable, and the named practitioners who will undertake the work. No marketing decks, no padding, no hidden assumptions.

Step 3

Design & bespoke build

Your bespoke software is engineered in‑house in Belfast, with weekly demonstrations, a live staging environment from the first week and direct access to the engineers writing the code. Senior craftspeople remain on the keyboard for the duration — not only at the kick‑off meeting.

Step 4

Launch, handover & ongoing care

A considered launch, comprehensive documentation, hands‑on training for your team and a thirty‑day warranty as standard. We then look after your bespoke build for as long as you wish — with a standing invitation to return for further work, which most clients do.
